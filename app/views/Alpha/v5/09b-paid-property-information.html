What's freehold?

You own a freehold property until you sell it, give it away or it otherwise passes to someone else.

Most houses are freehold. Most flats are leasehold. That means someone else owns the freehold for the building the flat is in.

If you want to know who owns a particular flat, you probably need the leasehold instead.

Restrictive covenants are contracts between two landowners. One landowner promises the other landowner not to carry out particular acts on their land.
