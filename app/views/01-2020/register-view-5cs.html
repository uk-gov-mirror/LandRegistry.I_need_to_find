Property information

Property description

5 Clinton Street, Exeter, EX4 1AX

This is how HM Land Registry have described the property in the register

How to update the property description

Write to us or complete form AP1 (link opens in new tab) to update the property description. For example, to change the property’s name.

You’ll need to send evidence from the local authority or Royal Mail Address Maintenance.

There is no fee for this application.

Where to send your application
HM Land Registry Citizen Centre
PO Box 74, Gloucester, GL14 9BB

Details of rent charge

This property has a rent charge of £100 payable on the 25th December.

This was created by a transfer dated 27 March 1986 made between The spitalfields Historic Buildings Trust and Henry Sackville Barlow. The registered rentcharge is charged upon and issued out of the Freehold Land shown and edged with red on the plan of the above title filed at the registry being 27 Fournier Street, Tower Hamlets, London.
