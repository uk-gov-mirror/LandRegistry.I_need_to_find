Use this service to get information about a property in England and Wales, even if you don't own it.

This page is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg)

Free information

You can view:

  • the full address
  • price paid history- you'll only get this if it was bought after April 2000, if more than £100 was paid or stated, or if HM Land Registry have recorded the price
  • general boundaries
  • property price trends for the local area
  • details of any planning applications
  • whether there are things you can or cannot do with the property (restrictive covenants and easements)

Need more information?

You can get more information about a property if it’s in HM Land Registry’s records.

You can buy:

  • the title register (official copy)
  • the title plan (official copy)
  • copy of title deeds

What will these documents give me?

These documents are £3 each.
