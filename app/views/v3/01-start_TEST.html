Use this service to get information about a property in England and Wales, even if you don't own it.

Free information

HM Land Registry holds records about most property or land sold in England or Wales since 1993. We can give you some of this information for free. This includes general boundaries (title extent) for freehold properties, price paid history and planning application details.

We can also tell you if the are any more entries in the register that may affect the use of the property - for details you'll need to pay a fee.

What information can I get for free?

You can view:

  • the full address
  • price paid history- you won't get this if it was bought before April 2000, if less than Â£100 was paid or stated, or if HM Land Registry have agreed not to record the price
  • general boundaries (title extent)
  • property price trends for the local area
  • details of any planning applications
  • if there are any other entries in the register that affect the use of the property

We may have more information about this property if it's been recorded in the register.

Premium information

You'll need to pay a fee to view more information recorded in the register. For example, title number, contact details for the owner and details of any other entries in the register that may affect the use of the property.

What information do I need to pay for?

You can view:

  • if there's a mortgage and who the lender is
  • contact details for the legal owner
  • details of any other entries in the register that affect the use of the property
  • the general boundaries (title extent) for leasehold properties
  • a copy of the register

You can:

  • print a copy
  • download a copy
  • forward a copy to an email address
