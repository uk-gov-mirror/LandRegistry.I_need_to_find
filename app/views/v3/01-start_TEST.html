Use this service to get information about a property in England and Wales, even if you don't own it.

Free information

HM Land Registry holds records about most property or land sold in England or Wales since 1993. We can give you some of this information for free. This includes, title extent, planning application details and if the are any legal constraints on the property.

What information can I get for free?

You can view:

  • the full address
  • price paid history for the property- you won't get this if it was bought before April 2000, if less than Â£100 was paid or stated, or if HM Land Registry have agreed not to record the price
  • property price trends for the local area
  • general boundaries (title extent)
  • details of any planning applications for the property
  • if there are any other entries in the register that affect the use of the property

We may have more information about this property if it's been recorded in the register.

Premium information

You'll need to pay a fee to view more information recorded in the register. For example, title number, details of any legal constraints on the property and title extents for leasehold properties.

What information do I need to pay for?

You can view:

  • contact details for the legal owner
  • if there's a mortgage and who the lender is
  • details of any other entries in the register that affect the use of the property
  • the general boundaries (title extent) for leasehold properties
  • a copy of the register

You can:

  • print a copy
  • download a copy
  • forward a copy to an email address
