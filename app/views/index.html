Search for land and property information Providing the right land or property data, at the right time, in the right way

By improving or replacing our current land or property information services - we're testing the assumption that we'll help to:

  • improve customer experience
  • reduce the number of calls to our citzen support centre
  • reduce technical debt

    Start Round 2 prototype - September 2019

    View Round 2 design decisions (opens in new tab)


    Things we wanted to find out included:

    • could users find the results they need even if the addresses appear to be the same? Search by street name - use Peel Street, choose Peel Street Runcorn, then 2 Peel Street, Runcorn.

    • could users find the result they need in a long list of results? Search by postcode - use EX4 1AX, choose 35 Clinton Street, Exeter, EX4 1AX.

    • what were users expecting to see if we don't have any information for a property they're looking for? Search by street name- use Peel Street, choose Peel Street Runcorn, and then 3 Peel Street, Runcorn.

    • could users navigate between pages of results? Search by street name or postcode - use Peel Street, choose Peel Street Runcorn, then 1,2 or 3 Peel Street Runcorn or EX4 1AX and 35 Clinton Street.
